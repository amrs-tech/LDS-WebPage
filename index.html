About LDS

The Literary and Debating Society, since 1948

Government College of Technology is one of the prestigious Engineering institutions in the state whose existence dates back to 1945. There are many clubs in the College among which the Literary and Debating Society holds a unique place.

Our constant pillar of Support - Anbazhagan Vijay
"The bridge that carries us over is praiseworthy"
Dr. Anbazhagan Vijay is the staff co-ordinator of the Literary and Debating society since many years. He is an Assistant Professor from the department of English, Government College of Technology, Coimbatore-13. He has been mentoring the club with his valuble ideas, experience sharing, moral support and rapport since the day he took charge of being a part of LDS. With many years of teaching experience as an English faculty and being a personal lover of literature, art and music, Professor Vijay is one person LDS is booned to have. If LDS enkindles with the same spirit in addressing and developing the passion for Literature since its start, a huge credit goes to his efforts that keeps the club fueled.

LDS is a group of like minded young passionates who work for a common cause of promoting english literature activities within the college. Besides literary arena, LDS offers platform for fellowship, personality grooming, public speaking and self development. LDS has been functioning since 1948 and till date never flagged down in the zeal to address the literary love amongst techies and engineers! Apart from many awards and achievements in lit fests ,LDS holds to its credit, a huge list of alumini who have reached great heights. LDS was, is and will remain an integral part of the GCTians with little literature enthusiasm.

About BRAINSTRAIN

Briefs and Crisps about BrainStrain



Introducing our Guest Speaker ! Nandhika Nambi, the author of the teen novel ‘UNBROKEN’ is a medical aspirant currently in her third year. Her book ‘UNBROKEN’ has been declared as one of the ‘memorable books of 2017’ by Amazon. She has already written 2 novels and ‘UNBROKEN’ is her third one. Apart from writing, she is also a Bharatanatyam dancer. To know more about her, make your presence at Brainstrain on Sept 15, 2017.

BrainStrain is a literary extravaganza organized by the Literary and Debating Society for the students. Hailed as the birthplace of talent, this event is an amalgamation of talent and fun.
Students from the top schools of Coimbatore battle it out in the biggest stage of them all - BrainStrain. This time it's specially for College Students !

Currently in its 40th edition, BrainStrain has over the last three decades, churned out lots and lots of winners who have made it big in life. Full of fun, frolic and celebrations - This is what we promise you ! And oh yeah, loads of memories too.

Take the plunge ! BrainStrain 2017 awaits you !
